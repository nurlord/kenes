Our Team

Ms. Aray Kasabekova

Director

A trade negotiator with 20 years of experience in Kazakhstan’s Government, she led WTO accession talks and represented the country in negotiations for the Eurasian Economic Union and the EU–Kazakhstan Partnership Agreement. From 2016 to 2019, she served at Kazakhstan’s Permanent Mission in Geneva, representing the nation at the WTO, UNCTAD, and ISO.

Mr. Roman Romashkin

Economist

An expert with 20 years of experience in trade, economic integration, food security, and agriculture. He has taught and conducted research at Moscow State University for over 15 years and participated in projects with the World Bank, OECD, EU, OXFAM, IFPRI, ICTSD, and UNCTAD/WTO.

Ms. Saltanat Jambylbek

Lawyer

A lawyer with 15 years of experience in corporate, labor, and business law, advising clients on corporate and regulatory matters in Kazakhstan. He has extensive expertise in contracts and legal documentation, with experience at KazTransGas, Nazarbayev University, and the Astana International Financial Center.

Ms. Asiya Ashigaliyeva

International Lawyer, Trade negotiator

With 18 years in Kazakhstan’s Government, she specializes in trade policy, lawmaking, and international negotiations. She contributed to WTO accession, major trade and investment agreements, and represents Kazakhstan in the OECD Investment Committee.
